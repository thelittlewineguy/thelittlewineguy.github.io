Wine Transportation Analysis: Metrics Between Napa and Sonoma and Bordeaux

France Metrics U.S. Metrics

Overview:

By Dan Ceresia, Sara Stapleton, Sam Tesconi, Bradlee Van Pelt

In this publication we will compare and contrast the systems for wine transportation resulting from the distribution models (2 tier/ 3 tier), including sustainability impacts. To focus our scope, we have limited our US Case study to California given all of the variations in state law.

MORE ON US SYSTEMS


We will also compare and contrast the systems for transportation for labor forces (e.g. delivering people as a resource and related requirements, such as housing in California and requirements in Bordeaux relating to continuity of first growth teams). For this we have limited our case study to Napa and Bordeaux so that we could immerse ourselves into experience of Bordeaux and compare to Napa which we believe is the most comparable US Market.
